Trip to Maui 2018

My wife and I took a trip tp Maui Hawaii for our honeymoon in 2018. While we were there we were blown away by the beaty of the island.

The Blow Hole

One the first day in Maui we went to what is described as "The Blow Hole". It was a hole in a rock by the ocean, that when waves wash up into it, it blows water through the hole that is in the rock. Learn more about the Blow Hole!

Food

The Food in Maui, was delicious. It was familiar to food we eat on the "Main Land", but had certain differences. Our favorite food on maui that we did not expect to like was the spam fried Rice. Another local favorite is Loco Moco.
